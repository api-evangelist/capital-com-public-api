RENK Group AG, a German transmission and drive systems manufacturer, reported record Q1 2026 order intake of 582.3 million EUR, surpassing analyst expectations and bolstering its 6.9 billion EUR order backlog. The stock has experienced volatility following a major shareholder's May block sale that increased free-float supply, though elevated European defence budgets continue supporting the company's structural growth prospects. Analyst targets range from 63-72 EUR per share, with the stock trading around 53 EUR as of early June 2026.
