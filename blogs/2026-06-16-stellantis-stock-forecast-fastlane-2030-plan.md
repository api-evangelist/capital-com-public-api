Stellantis' 2026 outlook is shaped by its EUR 60 billion FaSTLAne 2030 plan, which targets revenue of EUR 190bn and a 7% operating margin by 2030, alongside Q1 2026 adjusted operating income of EUR 1bn (a 194% year-over-year improvement). The stock trades at EUR 6.12 in mid-June 2026, down roughly 67.8% over two years, with analyst price targets spanning EUR 4 to EUR 12.50 and a 25% US tariff on EU auto imports as a key headwind.
