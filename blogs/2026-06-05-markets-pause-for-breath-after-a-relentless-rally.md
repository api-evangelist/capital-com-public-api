Global equity markets are pulling back from recent highs after an extended rally, with the Nasdaq 100 showing signs of fatigue as investors become more selective about whether current prices are justified. While earnings remain strong and AI investments continue supporting valuations, the upcoming US jobs report could significantly influence market direction. The report will determine whether growth fundamentals support existing valuations or whether economic concerns may emerge.
