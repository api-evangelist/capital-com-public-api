Markets are rallying on expectations of a US-Iran peace agreement and exceptional AI-driven earnings growth, with AI and strong earnings continuing to drive equities higher. The US leads globally due to semiconductor demand and technology sector profitability. However, elevated valuations, stretched positioning, and potential inflationary pressures from resource competition could limit the rally's sustainability.
