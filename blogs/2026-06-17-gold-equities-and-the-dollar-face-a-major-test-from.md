Markets are preparing for Federal Reserve Governor Kevin Warsh's first press conference, which will set the trajectory of interest rates. The article examines how his messaging could impact equities, gold, and the US dollar, with a patient tone supporting a stock recovery and stabilizing gold, while a hawkish stance emphasizing persistent inflation risks could pressure valuations.
