NVIDIA trades at $214.80 as the company unveiled its RTX Spark consumer PC chip at Computex 2026, marking entry into the AI PC market. The semiconductor giant reported Q1 fiscal 2027 revenue of $81.62 billion with Q2 guidance at $91 billion, both exceeding analyst expectations, while analyst price targets ranged from $280 to $425. Key risks include elevated valuations, US export controls on China chip shipments, and intensifying competition from established players in the consumer PC segment.
